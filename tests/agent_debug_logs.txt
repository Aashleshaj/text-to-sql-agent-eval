QUESTION: Which album has the highest total unit price across its tracks?
RAW AGENT RESPONSE: The album with the highest total unit price across its tracks is "Greatest Hits" with a total unit price of 56.43.

This makes sense because the unit price of each track is typically 0.99, and "Greatest Hits" appears to have the most tracks of any album in the database, resulting in the highest cumulative price when all tracks are added together.
EXTRACTED SQL: 'SELECT 
    a."Title" AS AlbumTitle,
    SUM(t."UnitPrice") AS TotalUnitPrice
FROM Album a
JOIN Track t ON a."AlbumId" = t."AlbumId"
GROUP BY a."AlbumId", a."Title"
ORDER BY TotalUnitPrice DESC
LIMIT 1'

QUESTION: Which playlist contains the most tracks?
RAW AGENT RESPONSE: Based on my analysis of the Chinook database, I can now answer your question:

The "Music" playlist contains the most tracks with a total of 3,290 tracks.

This is determined by joining the Playlist and PlaylistTrack tables, counting the number of tracks associated with each playlist, and ordering the results in descending order. The "Music" playlist has the highest count with 3,290 tracks, significantly more than any other playlist in the database.
EXTRACTED SQL: 'SELECT 
    p."Name" AS PlaylistName,
    COUNT(pt."TrackId") AS TrackCount
FROM Playlist p
JOIN PlaylistTrack pt ON p."PlaylistId" = pt."PlaylistId"
GROUP BY p."PlaylistId", p."Name"
ORDER BY TrackCount DESC
LIMIT 5'
